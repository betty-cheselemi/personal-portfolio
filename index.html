Projects

MY FIRST Project

This project explores how technology can enhance sustainable waste management through vermicomposting the process of using earthworms to convert organic waste into high-quality compost. Using sensors, microcontrollers (like Arduino or Raspberry Pi), and IoT connectivity, this smart system monitors key environmental conditions such as moisture, temperature, and pH levels within the compost bin. When the system detects suboptimal conditions for the worms or composting efficiency, it automatically adjusts settings (e.g., moisture levels via a water pump) or sends real-time alerts to the user via a mobile app or dashboard. The goal is to make vermicomposting more accessible, efficient, and scalable especially for urban households, schools, or small farms by reducing manual maintenance and ensuring a healthier composting process. This project sits at the intersection of agritech, environmental sustainability, and automation, reflecting my passion for using tech to solve climate and waste management challenges.
